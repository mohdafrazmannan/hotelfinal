About Us

Established in 2017, Hyderabadi Dum Biryani brings you the authentic taste of royalty. Our signature biryani is cooked the traditional and authentic way on a wood fire, imparting a rich, smoky aroma. This time-honored technique ensures every grain of basmati rice is perfectly infused with our secret blend of exotic spices. We carefully select the finest ingredients and slow-cook our meals to perfection, giving you an unforgettable culinary experience. Come and enjoy the warmth of our hospitality and the magic of our traditional delicacies that keep our guests coming back for more.
